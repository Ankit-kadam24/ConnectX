Why ConnectX
Networking without the baggage

Most platforms demand your data before you can say hello. ConnectX flips that model entirely.

🔒

Zero Personal Information

No email, no phone number, no real name. Just pick a User ID, set a password, and you're in. Your identity is entirely yours to define.

⚡

Real-Time Messaging

Once connected, chat instantly with your network. Messages are delivered live via WebSocket — no refresh needed.

Ephemeral by Design

Your chats disappear when you log out. Every session is a clean slate — what happens on ConnectX, stays on ConnectX.
